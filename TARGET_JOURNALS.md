## Why Free?

- **₹40,000 budget** (~$475 USD) eliminates all MDPI journals (CHF 1,600–2,600 / ₹1.4–2.3L) and most Elsevier/Springer OA journals.
- **Diamond OA journals** are funded by institutions, societies, or government grants — authors pay nothing.
- **Subscription-model journals** (e.g., Precision Agriculture) are free for authors when you choose the subscription publishing option (no OA fee required).

## Important Notes

1. **Acceptance rates** for Diamond OA journals are not publicly published. However, Diamond OA journals funded by institutions/societies tend to be **more selective** (not less) than APC-funded journals, because they don't rely on acceptance volume for revenue. Expect **30–50% acceptance** for the top picks.

2. **Artificial Intelligence in Agriculture** is the strongest option by far — Q1 Scopus, CiteScore 23.0, and completely free. It's a top-tier journal in the field. Expect rigorous peer review.

3. **Smart Agricultural Technology** is newer and may be more receptive to novel methodology papers (sensor degradation + ML pipeline).

4. **Precision Agriculture** (Springer) — free via subscription model, but highly competitive (~25% acceptance). Premium venue if you can get in.

5. **JSIR** (India) — published by CSIR, India's premier research institution. Free for Indian researchers. Good for national visibility.

6. **Check for special issues** — many of these journals have open special issues on "AI in Agriculture" or "Precision Farming" with faster turnaround.
