## Submission Tips

1. **If budget is $0:** Target Artificial Intelligence in Agriculture, Smart Agricultural Technology, J. Saudi Society of Agricultural Sciences, or Precision Agriculture — all free.
2. **If you need fast publication:** MDPI journals (Agronomy, Sensors) review in 2-4 weeks but charge ~$2,200-2,900 APC.
3. **If you want maximum prestige:** Computers and Electronics in Agriculture (free subscription mode) or Precision Agriculture (free). Longer review but higher impact.
4. **Emphasize in cover letter:** Leak-free methodology, dual-dataset design, sensor degradation novelty, BalWeightWrapper, and practical deployment guidance. These are the paper's differentiators.
5. **Check for special issues:** Many of these journals have open special issues on "ML in Agriculture" or "Precision Farming" with faster turnaround.
